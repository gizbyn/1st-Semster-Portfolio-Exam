In this theme, my main aim was at understanding the concept development using video, dramaturgy, film language and media elements and recording and editing techniques.

Work process
These projects are going to have one description because they are parts of one group project. At the beginning the problem we had to deal with was introduced: The Copenhagen Municipality wants to focus on biking as a good choice for getting around. They would therefore like newcomers to have access to a site (which has to follow the Copenhagen Municipality Design Guide) with information and an instructional video about the biking rules in Copenhagen. First of all, we created a team canvas (which you can see at the top) which was very important to us as a group. We decided on our common and personal goals, some rules and responsibilities. Then, after discussing a group work, we moved on to the actual story - we made a treatment (short but important information about the movie) which was later changed. A storyboard making followed (see on the top), then making of a moodboard and a style tile. Afterwards, we created an XD prototype (see on the top) of our websites according to the style tile and finally a website (see on the top) where we uploaded all our videos which we have shot and edited in a meanwhile.

My reflections
I would say that I really appreciate being part of this project. Especially, the new skills which we have learnt including editing videos in Adobe Premiere Pro. I will not lie, we faced some problems and challenges. Amongst the most serious ones are video editing, different opinions on the design of the website and not enough time for the production.

Skills gained
The skills I gained during this project covers video editing skills (Adobe Premiere Pro), film language and dramaturgical elements.
