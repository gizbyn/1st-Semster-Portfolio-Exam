Behind About

... outrageous, original, young, free, crazy, positive.

Who am I?

This is a very good question. Well, let's see what the answer is, right? It may sound like a cliché, but I believe that it describes me. I am beloved. Also a Multimedia Designer. A perfectionist. A beauty lover. A musician. A man. An open person. An artist. A child. A child of God. If you want to know more, do not hesitate to contact me.

What do I do?

First of all - I get inspired. That is an initial act. A process. A must. Everything I create starts with the touch of the muse. And then I design, code, ... Wait! Not so fast. Of course, before designing, coding or creating something, there is a research. Finding out what kind of options we have. Afterwards, tailoring the solution to the specific problem or the customer. Critical thinking. And a lot more.

Why do I do what I do?

Because I love it. Indeed. I love when something or someone looks good. I know that appearance is not everything, but it is important. But the thing, that really matters is inside. Beneath your skin and bones. What makes you breathe. What is inside your heart. And the beauty down there shines out and is visible. This is why I do what I do. Because we live in a consumer world. But the important is something that we cannot consume. While I was thinking about what to write, something came to my mind. I want to do what I do because of Him. I am a Christian and I believe that we have the loving Father. And I desire to be the vessel of His to reflect His beauty and love to the others. To the ones who need it.
